what to know about ordering

when do I need to place my order?

orders must be placed by thursday at 8pm to be baked and delivered that saturday. orders placed after thursday will be scheduled for delivery the following saturday.

do you deliver?

yes! local delivery (within 10 miles of provo, ut) is available on saturday mornings. delivery will be between 8am-11am.

can I pick up my order instead?

yes, you absolute sweetheart! you can choose pickup at checkout, and you'll receive the address and pickup time once your order is confirmed.

how do I pay?

all orders are placed and paid securely online through the cart. no cash necessary!

what’s in the bread?

three ingredients: organic bread flour, filtered water, and sea salt — naturally leavened with my 50 y.o. starter.
